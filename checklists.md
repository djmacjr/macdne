# Checklists

This page is a collection of decision frameworks used on macdne.com.

Nothing here is advice.  
These are personal tools designed to reduce emotion and increase consistency.

---

## 1) Crypto Panic Reversal — GO / NO-GO

### Market Structure
- [ ] BTC has not made a new low in the last 48 hours  
- [ ] BTC is trading sideways (range-bound)  
- [ ] Daily candles are smaller than panic candles  

### Volume & Volatility
- [ ] Highest volume day is in the past  
- [ ] Volume declining versus sell-off peak  
- [ ] Daily price range compressing  

### Relative Strength
- [ ] ETH no longer underperforming BTC  
- [ ] Risk assets stabilizing  

### Liquidations
- [ ] 24h liquidations down 50%+ from peak  
- [ ] Open interest flat or declining  

### Sentiment (Context Only)
- [ ] Fear remains elevated  
- [ ] No new systemic headlines  

**Decision:**
- 8–10 checks → GO (scale in slowly)  
- 6–7 checks → WATCH ONLY  
- ≤5 checks → NO-GO  

---

## 2) Daily Operating Rules

- Update once per day  
- No intraday decisions  
- Log observations in Notes  
- If not clearly a GO, do nothing  

---

## 3) Simple Position Rules (Optional)

Choose one approach and stick to it for 30 days.

### A) Calm / Diversified
- BTC + ETH core
- Add only after stabilization
- Scale in using 3 buys

### B) Small / Aggressive
- Fixed amount you can part with
- No leverage
- Accept volatility without reacting
